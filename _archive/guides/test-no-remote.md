# Rotator Cuff Repair Guide

## Overview
The rotator cuff is a group of four muscles and tendons that stabilize the shoulder joint.

## Surgical Technique
1. Arthroscopic approach preferred for small tears
2. Open repair for massive tears
3. Post-op rehab protocol: 6-12 weeks immobilization

## Key Points
- Success rate: 85-95% for small tears
- Recovery time: 4-6 months
- Complications: re-tear (5-15%), stiffness, infection
